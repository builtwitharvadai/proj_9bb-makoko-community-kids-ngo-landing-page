About Makoko Community Kids

Makoko Community Kids NGO is dedicated to transforming the lives of children in the Makoko community through comprehensive programs in education, healthcare, and community development. We believe every child deserves the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential.

Education

Providing quality education and learning resources to children in underserved communities

Healthcare

Ensuring access to essential healthcare services and promoting healthy living

Community

Building stronger communities through engagement and sustainable development
